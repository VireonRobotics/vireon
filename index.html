What we are building

We are pioneering robotics and IoT for a new era of security and emergency response.

RoadSure

A dashboard-mounted IoT safety device that uses AI and advanced sensors to detect attacks, accidents, and other road emergencies, then instantly triggers a distress alarm.

  • Automatic Emergency Detection: Automatically identifies critical events on the road.
  • Instant Distress Signal: Transmits a live GPS tracking link to emergency response agencies.
  • Live Vehicle Tracking: Monitor vehicles and high-value cargo in real-time.
  • Anti-Theft & Recovery: Ensures vehicles can be swiftly located and recovered if stolen.

Udide-6

An autonomous patrol robot. Its hexapod design provides stability and terrain adaptaility, It is equipped with lidar and dual spectrum cameras making it ideal for surveillance in hazardous or high-risk environments.

  • Multi-Terrain Hexapod Design: Navigates complex terrain with 6 agile, 3-DOF legs.
  • Advanced Sensor Fusion: Equipped with 3D LiDAR, proximity sensors, and dual-spectrum cameras.
  • Versatile Applications: Suited for patrol in mines, construction sites, and search-and-rescue.

Kiara

Our first prototype of a general-purpose companionship robot. Built on a bi-wheeled, self-balancing platform, Kiara is designed to interact intelligently and intuitively with users.

  • Dynamic Self-Balancing: Navigates smoothly with advanced obstacle avoidance.
  • Intelligent Communication: Integrated with an LLM and text-to-speech for natural user interaction.
  • General Purpose Platform: A foundation for future robots in assistance, education, and companionship.
